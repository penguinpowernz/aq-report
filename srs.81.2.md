# Shawn Ryan Show Episode 81.2 - Sarah Adams Interview
## Air Date: November 2, 2023

## Executive Summary

This episode features Sarah Adams, a former CIA officer who served in Libya during and after the September 11, 2012 Benghazi attacks. The interview provides an extensive analysis of the Benghazi attacks, revealing that it was a sophisticated al-Qaeda kidnapping operation directed by al-Qaeda leadership, involving approximately 150 terrorists with the goal of capturing Ambassador Stevens for prisoner exchanges. Adams exposes significant failures in the US response, including deliberate misinformation by CIA Chief of Base "Bob," the false narrative about an anti-Islamic video created by Jake Sullivan, and the Obama administration's decision to downplay the attacks while resources were misallocated to Tripoli. The interview details how key attackers remain at large, many in Afghanistan, and reveals that al-Qaeda has reconstituted to levels potentially exceeding pre-9/11 strength. Adams discusses her transition from CIA to the House Select Committee on Benghazi under Trey Gowdy, her discovery that the CIA was not actively pursuing Benghazi attackers, and her subsequent independent investigation. The interview concludes with warnings about current terrorism threats, Afghanistan as a terrorist safe haven, and concerns about terrorist infiltration through the southern US border and refugee pipelines.
